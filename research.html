Impact Cratering

I am interested in both the impact cratering process and using craters to understand the physical properties of projectile and the surface. My past research has included looking at the geometry of craters on Mercury to see what it can tell us about the strength of Mercury's crust and if impact velocity can play a role in final crater morphometry.

I have also perfomed computer simulations (using CTH) to model how target properties affect final crater morphology.

Surface Roughness

I use surface roughness (the change in vertical topography over a given horizontal scale) to quantify the properties of surfaces. I have used this method to understand the relative contributions of different geologic processes to generating the observed topography of Mercury.
